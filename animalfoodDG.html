Donate Food for Animals

Why donate food for Animals?

Animals rely on our support as they can't express their needs like we can. By donating food, we provide them with sustenance and care. It's our responsibility to lend a hand and ensure they have enough to eat. Together, let's make a difference in the lives of our furry friends.

Which Animals do we serve?

  • Cows and buffaloes: They are our prime focus. We feed them fresh-green grass, jaggery, special wheat-rotis, vegetables and seasonal fruits like watermelons.
  • Camels: We feed camels with grass and food similar to what we give to cows.
  • Goats: We feed goats with fresh-green grass.
  • Monkeys: We feed monkeys with bananas.
  • Rabbits: We feed rabbits with vegetables such as carrots. (Rabbits, not much in numbers, are fed occasionaly and based on donor's requests.)
  • Ants: We feed ants with special food made for them by grinding sugar and cocunut.
  • Crows: We feed crows with gaathiya (a chickpea-flour based food item).

How can you donate?

People usually donate money on special occasions like birthdays, marriage anniversaries, death anniversaries etc. Apart from special occasions, people may donate at any time they wish. Whether it be special occasions or a normal day, we volunteers are there for you. You can donate the food items which you wish to feed to animals or you may donate the money via UPI for the same. If the amount is substantially large, you can get it collected by our volunteer from your desired location.
